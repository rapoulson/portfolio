Of Lost Boys and Lion Kings

One Act

Category: Writing, Theater

Five writers were given the prompt, "Jane is a 38-year-old mother of two living in Ohio. Her kids, Sally (age 6) and Bobby (age 10), have just survived the car accident that instantly killed their mother. Jane’s estranged ex-husband, Jack, has now assumed custody of the children and moved to Ohio to keep the kids in their school. Jane’s sister, Lisa, lives in San Diego. Her parents live in Boca Raton. Jane was a nurse." They were then assigned to write a different scene from Jane's life to be presented in one evening, as a single, full length play, entitled "Dear Jane". "Dear Jane" was produced by ESPA at Primary Stages and is available for purchase on Indie Theater Now.
