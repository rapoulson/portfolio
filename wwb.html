Where We're Born

Assitant Director

Category: Theater

Small town rural America, class distinctions, sexual politics and love. "Where We're Born" explores the fact that sometimes in order to leave your home you have to destroy it. Lilly, a scholarship student at a prestigious liberal arts school, returns to her rural hometown on break. In an attempt to bring her new world and her old world together, Lilly breaks apart everything around her. "Where We're Born" was produced by Rattlestick Playwright's Theater and directed by Jackson Gay. The play is the third of Lucy Thurber's Hilltown Plays.
